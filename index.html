Yep, OK is a program for young adults who need support to relearn how to participate with confidence in everyday life. They may be struggling to re-engage with the world around them after COVID lockdown and conventional strategies may be inaccessable or ineffective.

Yep, OK combines group threapy facilitated by a Clinical Psychologists with coaching strategies to build competencies and confidence in achieviable and sustainable increments. It prioritises habit and skills formation by frequent practice of values directed actions.

The program combines group sessions with a Clinical Psychologist, individual coaching sessions, and group activities. Mutual accountability and commitments within the group are key factors.

The weekly schedule will consist of one group therapy session, one individual coaching session and one group activity.

The individual coaching session will start with rapport building, then move on to practice strategies to support the learning goals of that week. This might include advancing a hobby, movement, music making, bushwalks etc. Whatever is achievable and meaningful for that individual on that day.

We are seeking expressions of interest for participants in a four week pilot, followed by a month long hiatus then a six week program. Participants could opt-out after the initial four weeks, and some coaching availability will be available during the hiatus on a case-by-case basis. The cost will be $300 per week.
